How the Personal Information Detection and Deletion System works

We use state-of-the-art computer vision and Deep Learning algorithms to determine the face of the person in the picture. First, we extract the face of the image and normalize it. For that, we use the retina face.
The actual facial recognition is performed by a SFace which was shown to produce good results on various image analysis tasks.
